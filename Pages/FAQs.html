Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

We’re excited to have you here! To begin, click the "Join Trial" button and complete the form that appears. Our team will reach out to you within 48 hours to connect you with your ideal companion. If you need assistance, feel free to call us at +xx xxxxx xxxxx, Monday to Friday, from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

How are ALP-6 companions matched with Grandpals?

We take a thoughtful approach to understand the motivations and personalities of each ALP-6 member. We’ll maintain communication to ensure that you feel comfortable and satisfied throughout the process.

Is there a cost for this service?

Your initial sessions with an ALP-6 companion are completely free. If you enjoy the experience, you can opt for a paid subscription. If you decide not to continue, there will be no charges.

What safety measures are in place?

To ensure the safety of our Grandpals, all companions undergo personal interviews, complete in-house psychological assessments, and pass thorough background checks from law enforcement and third-party agencies. Additionally, there is a one-month probationary period for new companions.

Is the safety of ALP-6 companions also considered?

Absolutely! We prioritize safety for everyone involved. Seniors are vetted by our core team members and undergo basic background checks. Furthermore, ALP-6 companions can withdraw at any time if they feel uncomfortable.

What criteria do you use to select ALP-6 companions?

In selecting our companions, we evaluate their sincerity in responses and their genuine concern for the elderly.

Are ALP-6 companions compensated?

Yes, to ensure financial security and respect their time, ALP-6 companions receive competitive salaries. This enables them to dedicate their full attention to providing meaningful support to our Grandpals.
