From the Founder’s Heart: Uniting Generations!

It’s hard to pinpoint when my admiration for the elderly began, but it has always been a part of who I am. As I reflect on the friendships I’ve formed, many of my closest companions are those with silver hair and golden hearts. Their grounded perspectives and profound wisdom have enriched my life in countless ways, and I can’t help but see the youthful spirit that shines in their eyes when we share moments together. Intergenerational connections are incredibly special, perhaps even more so than any other relationships.

Yet, as I’ve spoken to more people, I’ve become aware that over 15 million elderly individuals in India live alone. While they may manage their daily needs, many face profound loneliness, whether due to the loss of loved ones or families that are simply too busy to connect.

Today, I’m fortunate to be joined by a passionate team of six empathetic graduates and professionals who, like me, have found joy and friendship in their relationships with older adults. We share a common vision: to foster these meaningful connections for those who yearn for companionship.

After an insightful beta phase filled with invaluable lessons, we are convinced that this is the path forward. We wholeheartedly believe in the love and comfort that come from bridging generations, and we are committed to nurturing these bonds with the utmost empathy and care.

With the trust and faith our Grandpals and their families have shown in us, I’m excited to say: The ALP-6 are on the way!

We are ALP-6

We strive to offer more than just a service; we aim to provide a truly enriching experience for our cherished Grandpals. Our mission is straightforward: to cultivate an environment where our Grandpals feel valued, heard, and supported, embracing them as if they were members of our own family.
