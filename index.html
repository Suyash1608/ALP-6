All the Mischief and Joy!

Energetic and Compassionate Companions for Seniors

We are a passionate team of young graduates who genuinely care for the elderly. We’re excited to build meaningful connections with our Grandpals, offering companionship and support—just like your grandchildren would!

Embrace the Joy of Connection

Whether it’s sharing fun conversations, reminiscing about the good old days, discussing life’s challenges, or enjoying snacks and tea, our ALP 6 companions are here to lend a listening ear and brighten your day.

Tech Guidance

Bridging the Digital Gap
Your ALP 6 companion is ready to spend as much time as you need, patiently guiding you through the world of smartphones, gadgets, social media, and online shopping. Whatever you want to learn, we’re here to help!

Strolling Together

Finding Delight in Every Step
We love to take walks and would be delighted to accompany you! Whether it’s visiting friends or enjoying a leisurely stroll in the park, our ALP 6 companions are always ready to lend a hand.

Daily Support

Assistance with Everyday Tasks
Need help with grocery shopping, picking up prescriptions, or running errands? Our ALP 6 companions are excellent shopping partners. Plus, having someone with you at the doctor’s office can make the wait much more enjoyable!

Explore New Horizons

Discover the Thrill of New Experiences
Join us for exciting outings! Whether you want to dine at a new restaurant, catch the latest film, learn a new hobby, or revisit an old passion, our ALP 6 companions are eager to take you on personalized adventures.
